The benefits of membership

This membership provides an array of exclusive benefits designed to enhance your experience and connect you with the museum's vibrant community. Enjoy unlimited admission to the museum, granting you the freedom to explore exhibitions and collections as often as you like throughout the year. Members also receive priority access to special events, including exhibition previews, lectures, and workshops, allowing you to engage more deeply with the art, culture, and history the museum has to offer. Take advantage of discounts on programs, gift shop purchases, and café offerings, making every visit even more rewarding. Additionally, members receive regular updates and newsletters, keeping you informed about upcoming events, exhibitions, and opportunities to participate in the museum’s initiatives. With the added flexibility of an additional membership card, you can easily share these privileges with a friend or family member, ensuring that your passion for discovery and learning can be enjoyed together. This membership is more than access—it’s an invitation to be part of a dynamic cultural journey.
